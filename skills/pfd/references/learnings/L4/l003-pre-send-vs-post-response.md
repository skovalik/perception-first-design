# Learning 3: Pre-Send vs Post-Response

**Insight:** Standard UX says "show your sources after the response" (attribution). PFD derivation says "show what the AI will see before the user sends" (legibility). Attribution is an L3 debugging tool. Pre-send legibility is an L1+L4 schema+trust tool. Different intervention points targeting different layers. Generalizes broadly: in communication and copy, what you show BEFORE the ask (setup, framing) is higher-leverage than what you show AFTER (explanation, justification).
