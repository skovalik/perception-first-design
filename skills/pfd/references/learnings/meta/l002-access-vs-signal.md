# Learning 2: Access vs Signal Mechanisms

**Insight:** PFD's layers naturally distinguish between "how do I access content?" and "how does the user know where content exists?" These are different design problems at different layers. Layer 1/2 handle signaling, Layer 3/4 handle access. Conflating them produces solutions that solve access without solving discovery. Applies equally to marketing: a CTA that's easy to click (access) is different from a page that makes the CTA feel like the obvious next step (signal).
