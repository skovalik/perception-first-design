# Learning 1: Generative vs Evaluative

**Insight:** When personas were given solutions first and asked to evaluate through PFD, they missed R2 (pre-attentive content signaling) entirely. When forced to derive bottom-up, R2 emerged as the consistent gap. PFD only generates non-obvious solutions when used bottom-up with constraint accumulation. Top-down evaluation refines but cannot escape the originating frame of the solution being evaluated.
